Introduction

Building Excellence Since 2020

ALKNAN AL MOTAHEDA Company offers world class, premium, and high-end quality of cleaning services and its maintenance in Kuwait. We first understand the need for the building and then draw up the detailed plan to undertake the work. Our staff is trained to use different equipment's required for facade cleaning such as scaffolding, spider man technique (hanging rope), cradle, platforms etc.

ALKNAN is one of the most respected building cleaning, electric scaffolding rental & maintenance, and general building maintenance companies in the GCC. In cooperation with our clients, we deliver pioneering projects that achieve long-term progress and economic growth thanks to the attention to the smallest details. ALKNAN is able to provide all clients with an integrated service in all their needs, along with a complete management of all internal and external maintenance and cleaning services for buildings.

Since 2020, we have completed more than 110 exceptional projects in several locations in Kuwait and UAE, sparking a passion for excellence. We operate through four main services:

  • General maintenance; rental & maintenance of electric scaffolding; external & internal
  • Cleanliness of projects and buildings.
  • ALKNAN's experience in cleaning and maintenance includes large-scale, low-rise and highrise
  • Buildings, residential and commercial buildings. ALKNAN also has extensive experience in the
  • Field of electric scaffolding rental and maintenance.

Our company may seem young, but it is always looking for leadership and a constant focus on our values, the core of which are safety, quality, ethics and integrity. These values are what we believe in, what we expect, what we deliver, and what we live.

With a dedication to being a socially responsible company, all of ALKNAN's operations and ethos focus on operating in a mindful manner.

Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Every Need

HIGH ACCESS CLEANING

At ALKNAN AL MOTAHEDA Company we have the ability to carry out bespoke cradle window cleaning for your specific building, ideal for if your building has a curved glass edge or is of any other irregular size or shape. We can design and develop cradles to be the perfect fit for your specific building. Our cradles are built to the highest quality, allowing for seamless movement when scaling the building, minimising the risk of damage to the building's exterior.

SPIDERS

ALKNAN AL MOTAHEDA Company spiders allow us to work in areas that would be inaccessible or too expensive to get to safely. Our team of specialist tradespeople, builders and engineers reach the trickiest of spots to get the job done, no matter what the task.
